U.S. Hotels, Inc. – A Legacy of Excellence

U.S. Hotels, Inc. has been a leader in the hospitality industry since 1995, building a legacy of acquiring, renovating, and developing high-quality hotels across the United States. Our story began with the acquisition and renovation of the Vicki Villa Motel in Elizabeth City, NC, and has since grown to encompass a diverse portfolio of properties operating under a variety of respected brands.

Our Growth Strategy

  • Strategic Acquisitions & Renovations: We specialize in identifying properties with potential and transforming them into modern, comfortable accommodations. Our expertise includes converting properties to different brand affiliations, maximizing their value and appeal to travelers.
  • New Hotel Development: We are committed to building new hotels that meet the evolving needs of today's travelers, incorporating modern amenities and sustainable practices.
  • Dynamic Portfolio Management: We regularly evaluate our holdings, strategically selling properties to optimize our portfolio and reinvest in new opportunities.
  • Brand Partnership: We proudly operate under well-known and trusted brands, including Marriott, IHG, Choice, Wyndham, and Best Western, catering to a wide range of travelers and budgets.

From small motels to large hotels, U.S. Hotels, Inc. is dedicated to providing exceptional guest experiences. We believe in creating comfortable, welcoming environments and are committed to the communities we serve. Our ongoing growth and success are a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and guest satisfaction. We look forward to continuing to expand our portfolio and provide travelers with exceptional hospitality for years to come.
